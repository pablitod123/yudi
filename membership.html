Membership Rules - 50% Attendance Minimum

You must be present at a minumum of one-half of each semester's offered lecture or speaker events to become a member. You must also compete in the YUDI competition to remain eligible. If you have any questions, please email yaleudi@gmail.com.

Attendance Tracking

Go to a QR Code Generator and create a QR code with your first and last name. In order for your attendance to be counted you must have your QR code scanned at each YUDI event.
